02

Capacity & Fuel Mix Analysis

Queue timing · Technology mix · Cross-market comparison

When are projects entering the queue, and how large are they?

Each bubble is one queue entry. Use this view to spot clusters in queue timing, large outlier projects, and which fuels are driving visible queue activity.

Why this matters: This helps developers see when market activity accelerated and whether the visible queue is dominated by smaller distributed projects or larger utility-scale entries.

What technologies dominate the selected market?

View annual queued capacity by fuel type or switch to a share-based view to see how concentrated the selected market is by technology.

Why this matters: This helps developers understand whether a market is leaning toward solar, wind, storage, hybrid, or thermal buildout and how that mix is changing over time.

How does the selected market compare over time?

Switch between total capacity, project counts, and average project size. When a specific ISO is selected, this view breaks out by state. When a specific state is selected, it stays scoped to that state.

Why this matters: This lets developers compare market size, queue crowding, and typical project scale across years, regions, and technologies using a single flexible comparison view.
